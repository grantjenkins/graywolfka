Subscribe

Check out details on how to subscribe

Why subscribe?

So that you get a notification each time I release a new program (game, graphic, animation, etc.).

How to subscribe?

Go into the Tips & Thanks just below this program and find the thread titled SUBSCRIBE HERE

To subscribe, just post a single period (.) in the thread. Posting something under 3 characters will only be visible by yourself, so you won't spam others. Feel free to let me know that you subscribed in another thread.

How will you be notified?

When I release a new program you will get a notification the same as you do when someone comments on one of your programs, or when you get a new badge, etc. The notification will include a link to the Subscribe thread where you can click on the link to the program.
